Your Questions, Answered

We believe in complete transparency. Here are the answers to some of the most common questions we receive.

For optimal results, we recommend starting the process 8-12 months before your intended intake. This provides ample time for profile evaluation, test preparation (if needed), university shortlisting, meticulous document preparation (SOP, LORs), and submitting applications well before deadlines. Starting early also significantly increases your chances for scholarships, which often have earlier deadlines.

Our shortlisting process is a blend of art and science. We begin with our AI-powered tool that analyzes your GPA, test scores, work experience, and budget. Then, our experienced counsellors add the human element. We consider factors like course curriculum, faculty research, internship opportunities, and your long-term career goals. We create a balanced list of 'Ambitious', 'Target', and 'Safe' universities to maximize your admission chances.

No ethical consultancy can guarantee admission or a visa, as the final decision rests with the university's admissions committee and the respective country's immigration authorities. However, we can promise that we will provide you with the most accurate information, meticulous application support, and expert guidance to build the strongest possible profile which significantly maximizes your probability of success.

Study abroad costs include university tuition, living expenses, health insurance, visa fees, and travel. These vary widely by country and university. We provide a detailed cost breakdown during our initial counselling. Regarding our fees, we operate on a transparent model with customized packages based on the level of service you require. We discuss all charges upfront during the free strategy call, ensuring there are no hidden costs.

Our support for financial aid is comprehensive. We maintain an extensive database of scholarships, grants, and assistantships. We help you identify opportunities you are eligible for, guide you in writing compelling scholarship essays, and ensure all financial documents are correctly submitted. We also provide guidance on education loans, connecting you with trusted financial institutions that offer favorable terms for international students.
