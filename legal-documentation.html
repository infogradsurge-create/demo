Last updated: October 2, 2025

  • GDPR (EU) data subject rights & consent

  • Indian IT Act, Consumer Protection, planned Data Protection
  • COPPA (minors under 13 parental consent)
  • PCI DSS (secure payment processing)
  • SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001 (security audits & controls)

2. Key Legal Protections & Anti-Exploitation Measures

  • Strict account creation and circumvention prevention
  • Identity verification for high-risk operations
  • Progressive penalties and legal escalation for abuse
  • Zero-tolerance: plagiarism, document fraud, and impersonation
  • Binding arbitration & class action waiver for all disputes
  • Explicit AI/algorithm recommendation disclaimers
  • Strong intellectual property (IP) clauses (DMCA, trademark, copyright)
  • Rate limiting and system usage monitoring
  • Refund/chargeback and financial fraud prevention
  • Multi-factor monitoring for system gaming and platform abuse

  • Force majeure and third-party service disclaimers apply
  • Progressive warnings, suspensions, and permanent bans
  • Right to pursue civil/criminal remedies for severe violations
